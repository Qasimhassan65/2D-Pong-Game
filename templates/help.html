How to Play

Welcome to the 2D Pong Game! Here are some quick instructions to get you started:

  • Objective: Score points by hitting the ball past your opponent's paddle.
  • Controls: Use the Up and Down arrow keys to move your paddle.
  • Scoring: There is a timer for every round, one with them most points at the end wins
  • Difficulty: The game speed increases with each point.

Good luck and have fun playing!
